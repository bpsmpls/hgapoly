• Confidential Corporate Campus

    As a company whose main priority is to connect the exterior and interior environments, light, views, transparency and access to nature are guiding principles of the overall design. HGA has mapped the metrics behind the guiding principles for the project to test and monitor their application throughout the design and construction process.

    Layering the guiding principles with the organizational, experiential, relational and operational data has helped us to view the principles from multiple angles to ensure that the space is meeting the demands of all who occupy it.
