• Confidential Case Study

    To respond to their strategic vision of aligning services and quality of inpatient environments across multiple campuses, a confidential client engaged HGA to develop prototypical private patient rooms and inpatient units that meet critical to quality criteria. This investigation measured the staff members' overall wellbeing as it relates to people, process, place, and technology. The findings from this investigation are helping to inform the design solutions for the new campuses. HGA went through a research-based process as well as created a beautiful human-centered, state-of-the-art design that is efficient to operate and accepts the future.
