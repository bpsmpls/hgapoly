• Kickoff

    • Determine the team members and bring entire team together.
    • Define the possible future scenarios.
    • Create a list of challenges to getting there.
    • Use DT problem statement activity to get a better understanding of the challenges.
    • Transform the problem into hypotheses to investigate and test.
    • Create a Critical to Quality list, prioritizing the guiding principles or needs.
    • Assign team members areas of research or focus.

  • Investigate

    • Work with Workplace strategy consultant on the…
    • Harvest the data already collected by poly.
    • Pinpoint any additional areas for consideration.
    • Assemble relevant benchmarking data.
    • Literature review of the knowledge base to understand best practices, leveraging other markets as necessary.
    • Develop a research approach for each hypothesis for the test lab.
    • Determine method(s), data points, sample size and timeline.

  • Generate

    • Brainstorm around design interventions.
    • Implement regular war room meetings with the full team to discuss solutions.
    • Apply findings from Investigate phase.
    • Create design schemes.
    • Seamlessly integrate poly’s culture, brand, workplace strategy and systems into a physical space that makes work and collaboration easy.

  • Iterate

    • Pilot test design through small buildouts in multiple locations if possible.
    • Gather updated user data based on the methods defined in the investigate phase.
    • Analyze data based upon initial hypotheses.
    • Refine solutions and retest if needed.

  • Design Guidelines

    • Create an interactive web-based tool that can be turned over to poly and easily modified over time.
    • The tool will articulate the design and standards by location and size.
    • The tool can also be a destination for the workplace planning and culture as it pertains to real estate.
    • If desired, the tool can continue to track data.
