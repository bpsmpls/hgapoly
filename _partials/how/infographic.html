• Kickoff

    1. Identify individual’s roles and solidify team unity.
    2. Define all possible scenarios.
    3. List challenges to end goal achievement thru Design Thinking exercise.
    4. Investigate and test problem hypotheses.
    5. Prioritize guiding principles/needs for a Critical-to-Quality list.
    6. Team members assigned to areas of research and/or focus.

  • Investigate

    1. Partner with Poly’s Workplace Strategist to absorb necessary information.
    2. Harvest Poly’s collected data and pinpoint additional areas for consideration.
    3. Literature review to generate report of benchmarking data and best practices.
    4. Apply HGA’s research approach to test each hypothesis.
    5. Collectively determine method(s), data points, sample size and timeline.

  • Generate

    1. Brainstorm around design interventions.
    2. Schedule regular war room meetings to specify solutions.
    3. Apply results from Investigate phase and create design schemes.
    4. Integration of culture, brand, workplace strategy, systems – to deliver an environment that fosters better productivity and collaboration.

  • Iterate

    1. Pilot test small buildouts in multiple locations (if possible).
    2. Collect user data based on methods from the Investigate phase.
    3. Analyze data against initial hypothesis.
    4. Refine solutions and retest if needed.

  • Design Guidelines

    1. Build a web-based tool that is interactive and can be easily modified over time by Poly.
    2. Benefits of web-based tool:
      a. articulate design standards by location and size
      b. destination for workplace planning and user culture within the space
      c. continue to track data.
