Ceiling Systems

The poly design standards can reimagine a typical ceiling using the already available kit of parts, but it will be important to have experts involved on the team who can point to the limitations and opportunities.

Furniture Manufactures

The furniture is a huge part of the built environment and having the manufacturers at the table will allow us to customize a solution for poly that can integrate your systems appropriately and be used globally.

General Contractors

Having multiple general contractors involved from the beginning will allow the team to troubleshoot prior to issues arising during construction. They can pinpoint integration, timing and availability questions as well as work with their subcontractors on innovative solutions that will work globally.

Personal Systems Engineering

The furniture and environment need to have flexibility built in for the personal systems as they evolve over time. Having this team integrated early will help give us the science and data behind these systems to allow the team to select the best products for long-term performance.

Enterprise Systems Engineering

Understanding the integration of the enterprise systems is key to the success of the project. The systems need to look and feel seamless and intuitive.
